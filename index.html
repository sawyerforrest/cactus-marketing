How it works

Simple to start.
Powerful at scale.

Cactus is designed to plug into your existing workflow with minimal setup — and grow with you as your volume increases.

01
Connect Easily

Cactus enables lassoed (integrated) and dark (shared credentials) carrier rate access depending on your WMS/TMS/OMS. 1–2 week onboarding process.

02
Ship Profitably

Your WMS or shipping platform calls Cactus for rates. We return the best options instantly and log everything to the Shadow Ledger.

03
Invoice with Confidence

Carrier invoices arrive, Cactus reconciles every charge, flags disputes, and generates a clean weekly invoice for each client — powered by AI.
